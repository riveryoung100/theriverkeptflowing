The Story Behind The River Kept Flowing

A journey through loss, perseverance, growth, faith, and becoming.

The Beginning

Every life is shaped by moments we understand immediately and moments whose meaning only becomes clear years later.

The River Kept Flowing is the documentation of those moments — the struggles, lessons, relationships, failures, victories, and discoveries that continue shaping who I am becoming.

Chapters Of The Journey

Chapter One: Foundations

Childhood, family, early experiences, the people who shaped me, and the lessons learned before I understood their importance.

Chapter Two: Searching For Success

The pursuit of achievement, competition, sales, entrepreneurship, and learning what success could and could not provide.

Chapter Three: The Mountain And The Fall

Building businesses, experiencing incredible highs, facing unexpected losses, and discovering what remains when everything changes.

Chapter Four: Rebuilding

Starting again, learning humility, rebuilding foundations, and discovering that losing everything can reveal what actually matters.

Chapter Five: The Desert Season

A season of separation, reflection, family, faith, discipline, and preparing for the next chapter.

Chapter Six: Building What Lasts

Creating a life centered around purpose, stewardship, service, relationships, and becoming the person I was created to be.

The Purpose Behind The Story

The goal is not to glorify the highs or hide the lows. The goal is to show what can be built when a person chooses perseverance.

Every scar carries a lesson. Every loss carries a responsibility. Every season has something to teach.

The river kept flowing because the journey was never finished.
