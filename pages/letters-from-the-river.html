Dad's Letters

A journey through memories, lessons, forgiveness, love, and understanding.

The Letters

Throughout my life, my father and I exchanged letters. Words written across seasons of distance, uncertainty, growth, and change.

For years, these letters have remained untouched — carrying memories, emotions, questions, and pieces of a relationship that shaped who I became.

This series is the process of opening that chapter, reading those words, and discovering what lessons remain.

Why This Matters

Some lessons are learned through success. Others are learned through loss.

Time is one of the greatest gifts we have, and the people we love are worth showing up for while we still have the opportunity.

The purpose of sharing this story is not to live in the past, but to understand it, learn from it, and carry those lessons forward.
